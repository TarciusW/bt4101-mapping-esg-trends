Chairman’s Message

主席致辞

On behalf of the Board, I am pleased to present the Sustainability 

Report of China Sunsine Chemical Holdings Ltd. (“China Sunsine”, 

together with its subsidiaries, collectively the “Group”) for the 

financial year ended on 31 December 2018 (“FY2018”).

The Group achieved unprecedented success in 2018, thanks to 

the Group’s consistent focus on environmental protection, safe 

production methods, as well as allocating huge investments in 

both manpower and facilities.

Environmental protection is the top priority of enterprises, as it is 

a basic guarantee for sustainable growth. Like all other chemical 

products, the rubber chemicals industry covers diverse fields 

such as nature, chemistry, environment, safety and humanities. 

While making great contributions to the society, the industry also 

assumes heavier social responsibilities.

As environmental protection regulations become stricter, and with 

people’s rising desire and emphasis on environmental protection, 

we must set higher standards for ourselves in environmental 

protection, safe production, technological innovation, and green 

production.

We will continue to reduce energy consumption, reduce, treat 

and recycle waste, and eliminate hazardous waste generation. 

In furtherance of these objectives, we will enhance employees’ 

awareness of environmental protection and safety, and implement 

strict rules and regimes to integrate sustainable development 

concepts into daily work routine.

While all these efforts have helped the Group in maintaining 

market leadership position amidst intense competition, we 

believe that we have a big role to play in creating more value for 

society. The Group is mindful of its social responsibility as well as 

stakeholders’ interests. The Group actively fulfills its corporate 

social responsibilities through contributions to various charities, 

poverty alleviation, scholarships, and job creation. The Group 

also encourages employees to achieve their self-worth and ideals, 

delivers greater returns for its shareholders, and shares the 

fruits of success and experiences with peers in the industry and 

stakeholders. All these go towards contributing to the building of a 

harmonious society.

“Enterprises will thrive by being thrifty, charitable, and steadfast 

in constantly reviewing past performance with a clear mind”. 

No matter how successful we are and what difficulties we are 

confronted with, we must keep a clear mind, remember the 

mission, and continue to remain true to our original mission and 

aspiration. Let us work together to create a better future!

Yours sincerely,

XU CHENG QIU

Executive Chairman

March 2019

Organisational Profile

公司简介

China Sunsine Chemical Holdings Ltd (“China Sunsine” or the 

“Company”, and together with its subsidiaries, collectively the 

“Group”) is a leading specialty chemical producer, and was 

listed on the Main Board of the Singapore Exchange on 5 July 

2007. Its main subsidiary, Shandong Sunsine Chemical Co., Ltd 

was established in 1977 and started off as a chemical plant. 

It has a total asset of RMB 2.75 billion, 2200 employees, 700 

professional technical staff over 40 years of development. There 

are four production bases - Shandong Sunsine, Weifang Sunsine, 

Sheng Tao and Guang Shun. The products include Rubber 

Accelerators, Insoluble Sulphur and Anti-Oxidant. Our products 

are widely used in and for the manufacture of rubber, tires, 

shoes, belts, pharmaceuticals and agrochemicals. Our products 

are exported to over 40 countries and regions. We are number 

one globally in market position based on total sales volume. 

We have over 1,000 customers, including the global top 75 

customers in the tire industry. Guided by our policy of “Adhering 

to technology, market-oriented focus and product quality for 

sustenance; Striving to open up new markets, actively developing 

new products, and improving the Group entirely”, we assume the 

leading role of developing the world’s rubber chemicals, rely on 

independent innovation to upgrade the industrial transformation, 

and strive to achieve energy-saving emission reduction and 

healthy and sustainable development. 

中国尚舜化工控股有限公司（简称“中国尚舜”或“公司”，

与其子公司一起合称“集团”）是一家专业化工企业，并于

2007 年 7 月 5 日在新加坡证券交易所主板上市。公司的主要

生产子公司 – 山东尚舜化工有限公司始建于 1977 年，前身是

国营山东省单县有机化工厂，至此报告发行时已经过 40 年的

发展，目前拥有总资产 27.5 亿元，员工 2200 多人，专业技术

人员近 700 人，辖山东尚舜、潍坊尚舜、盛陶化工、广舜热

力四个生产基地。公司产品主要有橡胶促进剂、防老剂、硫化

剂等三大类十几个品种，广泛应用于橡胶、轮胎、胶鞋、胶管

以及医药、农药等行业，出口 40 多个国家和地区，销量连续

多年位居全球第一，拥有全球 1000 多家的客户，包括服务超

过 65%	的世界前 75 强轮胎企业。这些都是由于我们秉承“坚

持科技兴化，坚持以市场为导向，以质量求生存，以经济效益

为中心，努力开拓新市场，积极开发新产品，全面提高企业素

质”的发展方针，以引领世界橡胶助剂发展为己任，依靠自主

创新引领产业转型升级，实现节能减排和健康可持续发展。

Hydrogen sulphide created during our production process is 

harmful to health and the environment. Our sulphur recycling 

facility has effectively extracted the sulphur from hydrogen 

sulphide for re-use in our production process, such as in the 

production of our thiazoles accelerator, MBT.

To effectively manage the hydrogen sulphide emission at our 

manufacturing facilities, we are the first company in China to 

invest more than RMB 30 million on a Super Claus furnace and 

CS2 recovery facility. Our conversion recovery rate is consistently 

above 99.5%.

The Group made further investments to enhance odour 

emissions control, as follows:

1. Implemented the “Feasibility Plan for Promoting Odour 

Control” published by the Chinese Academy of Environmental 

Sciences, where hydrogen sulphide generated should be 

categorised and analysed. RTO (Regenerative Thermal 

Oxidizer) incineration is used to treat highly concentrated 

hydrogen sulphide. RTO incineration plans will be screened 

and suitable plan is selected for implementation;

2. Installed a fully automated wastewater evaporator at MBT 

workshop to enhance wastewater management and recycling; 

3. Hydrogen sulphide from MBT workshop evaporation area was 

collected and treated using RTO incineration. The discharged 

toxic gas from 4020 small and high-grade oxidation area 

was collected using airtight method and treated using the 

spray absorption process. The DZ multi-effect Fenton change 

tank and the mother liquid open tank were sealed and RTO 

incineration treatment was adopted. The feeding ports in NS1, 

NS2 and CBS workshops were treated using ventilation, dust 

removal and absorption measures;

4. Installed a cryogenic recycling equipment in Shandong 

Shengtao Chemical Co,Ltd to recycle carbon disulfide used in 

our Insoluble Sulphur workshop. More than 2 tons of carbon 

disulfide were recycled every day, achieving both economic 

and environmental sustainability;

5. Establish a VOC (“Volatile organic compounds”) action plan, 

focusing on leakage detection and repairs as well as to recycle 

these VOC.
