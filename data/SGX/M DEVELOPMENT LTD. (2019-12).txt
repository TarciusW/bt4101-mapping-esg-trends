SUSTAINABILITY REPORT 

 

 

 

The Board of Directors (“Board”) of M Development Ltd (“Company”) refers to its announcements dated 

7 September 2017 and 7 December 2018 and wishes to informed Shareholders that the Group will be 

not releasing a sustainability report under Rules 711A and 711B of the listing manual (“Listing Manual”) 

of the Singapore Exchange Securities Trading Limited (“SGX-ST”) for the financial year ended  

31 December 2019, in view of the following reasons: 

 

(a) 

as announced by the Company on 7 September 2017, the SGX-ST has deemed the Company to be 

a cash company under Rule 1018 of the Listing Manual; and 

 

(b) 

as announced on by the Company on 7 December 2018, pursuant to Rule 1018(2) of the Listing 

Manual of SGX-ST, the Company will be removed from the Official List of SGX-ST and a cash exit 

offer in accordance with Rule 1309 should be made to Shareholders within 6 months. 

 

The Company will make further announcement(s) in the event of any material development in the affairs 

of the Group from time to time in accordance with the Listing Manual. 

 

 

BY ORDER OF THE BOARD 

M DEVELOPMENT LIMITED
